The Capital Ring is 78 miles of walking and running route circling inner London. I've been doing it in sections on weekend long runs.

It's a good excuse to see parts of the city I'd otherwise never visit on foot.

### Progress so far

Three sections down. The stretch from Richmond to Wimbledon is particularly good — open parkland, easy to navigate, and almost no road running.
